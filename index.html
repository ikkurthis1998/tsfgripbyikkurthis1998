GRIP

The Graduate Rotational Internship Program (GRIP) is an unique offer for students and recent graduates to experience and join TSF.

This program aims to enable students to be professionally capable, and entrepreneurial.
Apart from skill specific tasks, we encourage interns to build a credible professional profile.

Specializations (Job Descriptions)

Web Development

Mobile App Development

Data Science & Business Analytics

Content Development

Campus Ambassador

Digital Marketing

Talent Acquisition

IoT & Computer Vision

Benefits

  • Completion Certificate
    A certificate is provided after successful completion of one month of internship.

  • Recommendation Letter
    A letter of recommendation is provided to top performing interns based on various assessment parameters.

  • Opportunity to be a part of TSF
    An opportunity is given to best candidates and they are hired on a special hiring plan.

  • Workshops/Guidance
    Workshops/guidances are provided for personality insights, Resume & LinkedIn profile building, Scrum training Session, interview skills, further education, etc.

  • Networking Opportunity
    A networking opportunity as a global, multicultural & rare diverse working environment is available for interns.

More things to know...

  • Unpaid Internship
    This is an unpaid internship of a month during which you'll be trained, mentored and observed for the future role.

  • Own computers and internet
    You'll use your own computers with internet connections and coordinate with your team members through Whatsapp or Google Hangout calls, etc.

  • Flexible working hours
    During the course of the internship you will be expected to work from home with the flexibility to manage your time. The tasks assigned are easy and can be finished in your extra time.

Guidance and Mentoring

  • Scheduled Calls
    Regular contact with core team members via live online sessions.

  • Mentor Support
    Our mentors are there to guide and support you at any time. You can reach the mentors through email or whatsapp.

  • Discussion Forum
    You will be asked to join The Sparks Foundation Network group on LinkedIn. Here, your mentors and facilitators will answer your task queries.

  • On Demand Support
    If you feel the need, you can mail us for help. We will guide you to the correct resources.

Instructions for applying

  1. Read about the Specializations carefully and apply for the correct one.
    • Go through the brief Job Descriptions of the specializations, to make sure you have applied to or applying for the correct one.
    • The Internship Function will be mentioned on your GRIP Selection Certificate.
  2. Join our discussion forum - The Sparks Foundation Network group on LinkedIn.
    • It may take upto 2-3 days to approve your request.
    • This group will be used for important announcement from our team, post task related queries from interns and query resolutions from mentors.
    • If you do not have a LinkedIn profile, please create one. One of your tasks will be related to improving your personal brand online.
  3. Fill the Interest form with the Preferred Internship Specialization.
    • If you want to change your internship specialization, just refill the Interest Form before deadline. Your last response will be considered final if there are multiple submissions.
    • You will receive response receipt of the form after submission in your email. Open the email and simply FORWARD it to grip@thesparksfoundation.sg & tech@thesparksfoundation.sg WITHOUT changing the subject.
  4. Create profile on My Credible Info.
    • Create your profile and submit details asked in different sections. The details here will be used to verify your background.
    • Please read the Guidelines of My Credible Info.
    • Also read the FAQs for all doubts related to GRIP.
    • If you still have any doubts, write in TSF Network.
  5. GRIP Selection certificates are sent in the last week of each month after evaluating your profile.
    • You must complete all the steps above to get the Selection Certificate, even if you are selected on a job portal like Internshala.
    • There may be no communication or acknowledgement email from our end between your application and us sending you the selection certificate.
